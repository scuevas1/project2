Arepas con queso

Ingredients

  • 1 cup pre-cooked arepa flour
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1/3 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1/3 cup of queso fresco
  • 2 tbsp of butter
  • 1 tsp of salt

Instructions

  1. Combine arepa flour, warm water, salt, butter, and mozzarella cheese
  2. Once combined, knead the dough with your hands for about 4 minutes while moistening your hands with water
  3. Form 5 small balls with the dough, then flatten each ball with your hands
  4. Add butter to a pan over medium to low heat. Place arepas in pan and cook for about 3 and a half minutes on each side or until each side is fully cooked and crispy
  5. Once cooked, place the arepas on a plate and top each arepa with queso fresco and mozzarella cheese

Huevos con Chorizo

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 7 oz of Chorizo
  • 3 tbsp milk
  • Sour cream
  • Oaxaca cheese
  • Queso fresco
  • Corn tortillas

Instructions

  1. Cook the chorizo in pan over medium heat
  2. While the chorizo is cooking, beat the eggs and milk in the bowl
  3. Once chorizo is cooked add the eggs and milk mixture and put it on medium to low heat
  4. While that is cooking, put the tortillas in the microwave for 30 seconds to soften
  5. Once the eggs and chorizo are done cooking build the breakfast tacos. Add the eggs, chorizo, sour cream, oaxaca cheese and queso fresco onto the tortillas
