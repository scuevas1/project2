Three best cities to visit in Puerto Rico

Viejo San Juan

It is a very colorful and gorgeous part of the island. It is known for its amazing colonial architecture, cobblestone streets, and amazing culture.

It is also home to one of the most amazing landmarks, El Morro. A fort that the Spanish began to build in 1539 and it took over 200 years to complete.

Recommended time to spend in Viejo San Juan:

About 4 days is enough to explore the city and try the amazing food.

Culebra Island

It is a small island off the eat coast of Puerto Rico. Culebra is home to one of the most beautiful beaches, Flamenco Beach.

It is one of the most famous beaches in the world because of the beautiful white sand and turquoise water.

Recommended time to spend in Culebra Island:

About 2 to 3 days is enough to fully enjoy the island and get beautiful rest.

Rio Grande

Home to El Yunque a gorgeous Tropical rainforest in northeastern Puerto Rico. It is known for its amazing trails and waterfalls.

With the main trail taking you all the way up to the observation tower to overlook four types of the forest.

Recommended time to spend in Rio Grande:

With enough planning 2 days is sufficient to hike the trails and get rest.

Three best cities to visit in Belgium

Bruges

This is by far one of my favorite cities and it is located in northwestern Belgium. It is best known for its beautiful canals and gorgeous medieval towns.

While in Bruges it is important to see its famous landmark the Belfry of Bruges the medieval bell tower in the center of Bruges, Belgium.

Recommended time to spend in Bruges:

About 3 nights but mainly two full days to see the amazing art and architecture.

Brussels

This is such a gorgeous city and it is the capital of Belgium. While in Brussels a must-see is the Grand Palace located in the center of the city, it is a gorgeous landmark surrounded by many restaurants and stores.

Brussels is known for its incredible architecture and cuisine including the famous waffles and french fries.

Recommended time to spend in Brussels:

About 3 days is recommended to see the beautiful attractions and to try the delicious chocolates!

Antwerp

One of Belgium's largest cities and they are known for their diamonds. They even have an entire distric dedicated to them.

Just like the other cities Antwerp is famous for their Architecture like the Antwerp Central Station and Cathedral. The Groenplaats city square is a nice place with many restaurants, cafes, and shops.

Recommended time to spend in Antwerp:

About 2 days is recommended to see the amazing architecture, shops, and to see the diamond district.
