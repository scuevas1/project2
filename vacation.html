Three best cities to visit in Belgium

Bruges

This is by far one of my favorite cities and it is located in northwestern Belgium. It is best known for its beautiful canals and gorgeous medieval towns.

While in Bruges it is important to see its famous landmark the Belfry of Bruges the medieval bell tower in the center of Bruges, Belgium.

Recommended time to spend in Bruges:

About 3 nights but mainly two full days to see the amazing art and architecture.

Brussels

This is such a gorgeous city and it is the capital of Belgium. While in Brussels a must-see is the Grand Palace located in the center of the city, it is a gorgeous landmark surrounded by many restaurants and stores.

Brussels is known for its incredible architecture and cuisine including the famous waffles and french fries.

Recommended time to spend in Brussels:

About 3 days is recommended to see the beautiful attractions and to try the delicious chocolates!
